The composition terrain reaches ~+1.9km / ~-1km — ~8x outside the AABB. FIX: world_view const AABB_HALF_HEIGHT=4000; page AABB now -4000..+4000 vertical. Oversized AABB only costs a little over-draw, never a wrong cull. Human confirmed: terrain stays visible at all angles. m1_7c still PASS (render-only change).

I chased the character controller for too long; the systematic measurement (collision is fine) + the human's angle clue is what cracked it.
